Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes, you can cancel your plan whenever you'd like. You'll be switched to the free plan at the end of your billing cycle.
Is there a free trial available?
Absolutely! The "Start Here" plan is completely free forever. For the Startup plan, we offer a 14-day free trial with no credit card required upfront.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and wire transfers for Enterprise accounts.
Can I upgrade or downgrade later?
Yes, you can change your plan at any time from your dashboard settings. Prorated charges will apply immediately.
